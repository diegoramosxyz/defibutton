---
  title: "Spend"
  description: "How do I buy stuff with cryptocurrency?"
---

Spending your cryptocurrencies is not as easy as it should be, **yet**. Decentralized finance is a new technology and people are just starting to adopt it. It will take years, but eventually you will be able to transact your crypto cheaply and fast. This will totally depend on the willingness of people to accept cryptocurrencies as a medium exchange.
