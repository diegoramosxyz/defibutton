---
title: 'DeFi Pulse Index'
description: 'A market capitalization-weighted index for all of DeFi.'
---

The DeFi Pulse Index ($DPI) tracks the performance of decentralized financial assets across the market. It can be used to easily gain exposure to the best performing DeFi assets. 

The tokens included in this index are selected based on a criteria and are updated monthly. The DeFi Pulse Index aims to include DeFi projects with high usage and proven commintment to ongoing maintenance and development. This is index in mantained by real people and they make the adjusments to the index.
