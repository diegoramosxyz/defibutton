If you are an experienced investor you can profit from volatility by making riskier trades. Investor make risky trades because they often provide a higher return if successful.

Despite the fact that DeFi is only possible thanks to computers and the Internet, humans are usually the ones trading in the DeFi markets. As such, there are patterns known in traditional financial that also apply to DeFi. The patterns can be used to "predict" with some level of certanty how the value of an asset will perform over a set period of time.

## Buy low, sell high

Regardless of the potential for growth of a cryptocurrency you can trade it over a short periods of time and profit from high volatility.
