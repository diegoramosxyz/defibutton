## What is decentralized finance (DeFi)?

Decentralized Finance is a concept that refers to a collection of decentralized financial services that when combined can replace the current centralized financial system. There is no entity who determines what forms part of DeFi. The nature of this space allows anyone to create a financial service, thus people are free to determine which protocols to use to fulfill their financial needs.

## Why should I care about DeFi?

You should care about decentralized finance (DeFi) because it gives you absolute control over your finances. After the invention of Bitcoin, the first cryptocurrency, DeFi became the logical next step towards a better financial system. One that is open to every internet user in the world, has no "CEOs" or "presidents", has no intermediares, requires no permission to be used and cannot be censored.

DeFi will not instantly solve all your financial problems, but it will give you the choice to solve them on your own terms.
